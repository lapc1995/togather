Introduction
Gather a group on whatsapp
Get a nostalgic, creative, fun or challenging topic
Share it with your group
Create / capture / write / photograph
Repeat this every day
So when they get out, you can share all the love and support you wanted to give them in a diary full of your stories, thoughts and images.

Hi there!

We understand that you might be going through some difficult times while someone dear to you is fighting in the hospital. At these times it can feel strange that the world just keeps going without them taking part in it. You might feel like you want to do something for them, but few of us can. Therefore, we want to help to capture your experience of some everyday moments with the other people around you that feel the same. So, when your loved one leaves the hospital, you’ll have a diary of those days and thoughtful messages to share with them. Besides that, it might also take your mind of things while you are capturing some things that happen in your life as a group through a different lens. Have a look at ones we’ve made ourselves, and when you are ready to get started just follow the steps described below!
How it works
To Start
Create a group in Whatsapp with all the people that want to contribute to this diary.
How-to for Android How-to for iOS
Let everyone know what this group is about. You can use our example:
“Hi everyone, this group is a place where we can share things we would like to capture for [INSERT NAME] in a diary. Every day we will post a topic for inspiration what to message about today. If you have an idea for a topic feel free to share this. Also, if you just have something you want to share for the diary unrelated to the topic please do! As an opening of this group it would be nice to introduce everyone who is part of this, so post a picture of yourself, who you are to [INSERT NAME], and a short personal message for them. “
Now you are all set to share your daily entries for the diary.
Each Day
Visit this website to receive a new topic to share with the group. If you don’t like the randomly generated topic, then click the button to get a new one. When you are happy with the topic, then click to share it in you WhatsApp group.
You can also create your own topics for the diary. Just write about it and share it in your WhatsApp group as a message or photo.
TIP: make it easy to find your own topics in the end by starting the message with the words “today’s topic is...”
Get everyone to share their responses in the WhatsApp group and repeat this process every day for as long as you would like to keep the diary going.
To make the diary
When you are finished and want to turn your messages and photos into a diary for your loved one, simply export the chat log for the WhatsApp group and save it on your phone, computer or cloud storage (e.g. Google Drive, iCloud or Dropbox)
How-to export in Whatsapp
TIP: Anyone from the WhatsApp group can do this, so ask for help if you are having difficulties.
Come back to this website and upload your exported WhatsApp chat log (and media files). Don’t worry, your personal messages and media files are not being uploaded anywhere, everything is happening locally on your device – we don’t collect any personal data. This ensures your conversations and messages stay private and you have full control over who you want to share your diary with.
Your group chat will be loaded and presented back to you in our Diary tool. You will be shown a list of topics that were shared in the WhatsApp group, a you can select any others that we missed or create your own by describing the topic for that day.
For each topic you will be asked to select which messages you want to add to the diary for that day. Don’t worry if there is a day you didn’t submit anything, everyone deserves a rest, we will skip over those days and the dates will keep counting on.
When you have done this for every day of the diary you want to share, select the finish button and all your messages will be placed in the template and your diary will be ready for you to download so you can print it or share it with your family, friends and loved one.
