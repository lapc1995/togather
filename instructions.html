Instructions
VIDEO
#1 Get started
Create a group in Whatsapp all the people that want to contribute to this diary.
How-to for Android How-to for iOS
Let everyone know what this group is about. You can use our example:
“Hi everyone, this group is a place where we can share things we would like to capture for [INSERT NAME] in a diary. Every day we will post a topic for inspiration what to message about today. If you have an idea for a topic feel free to share this. Also, if you just have something you want to share for the diary unrelated to the topic please do! As an opening of this group it would be nice to introduce everyone who is part of this, so post a picture of yourself, who you are to [INSERT NAME], and a short personal message for them. “
#2 To-do Daily
Visit us here to receive a new topic each day.
If you are happy with the topic, then click to share it in your WhatsApp group.
When you don’t like the randomly generated topic, then click the button to get a new one.
Or create your own topics for the diary. Just share them in your WhatsApp group as a message or photo.
TIP: make it easy to find your own topics in the end by starting the message with the words “today’s topic is...”
Everyone can share responses in the WhatsApp group.
Repeat this every day for as long as you would like to keep the diary going.
#3 Assembling the diary
VIDEO
To turn your group chat on Whatsapp into a diary follow these simple steps:
Export the group chat, including media, and save it on your phone, computer or cloud storage. (e.g. Google Drive, iCloud or Dropbox)
< class="phone-image second" src="{{ '/assets/images/tap.svg' | prepend: site.baseurl }}"> How-to for Android
TIP: Anyone from the WhatsApp group can do this, so ask for help if you are having difficulties.
Come back to this website and upload your exported group chat to our Diary tool. (the photos, videos, gifs and the .txt file that you have saved during the previous step)
Don’t worry, your personal messages and media files are not being uploaded anywhere, everything is happening locally on your device – we don’t collect any personal data. This ensures your conversations stay private and you have full control over who you want to share your diary with.
Start by telling us who this diary is for to create a personalized document
Then check the names of all the contributors. We will show you a list of the names we found in your export. Change them to the names you would like to see in the diary.
Next we will show you the topics we could find in the group chat. If there are topics we missed you can select them from your messages, or write your own.
For each topic you will be asked to select which messages you want to add to the diary for that day. Don’t worry if there is a day you didn’t submit anything, everyone deserves a rest, we will skip over those days and the dates will keep counting on.
When you have done this for every day you want in the diary, click finish. All your messages will be placed in a template and your diary will be ready for you to download. You can print and/or forward it to share the diary with your family, friends and loved one.
