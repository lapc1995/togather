Togather explained
ToGather makes it easy to capture and share in the time spent apart from your loved ones.
Gather a group on Whatsapp
Get a nostalgic, creative, fun or challenging topic
Share it with your group
Create , capture, write, photograph and share with your group.
Repeat this every day
So when your loved one leaves the hospital you can share all the love and support you wanted to give them in a book full of your stories, thoughts and images.
